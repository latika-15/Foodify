Importance of Minerals

Minerals are essential nutrients our bodies need in small amounts to function properly. They contribute to various bodily processes, including:

  • Strong bones and teeth
  • Muscle function and nerve transmission
  • Fluid balance and blood pressure regulation
  • Energy metabolism and enzyme activity
  • Overall health and well-being

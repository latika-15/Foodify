Types of Carbohydrates

Simple Carbs

Sugars, readily absorbed for quick energy.

Complex Carbs

Starchy foods, provide sustained energy and fiber.

Fiber

Promotes digestion, gut health, and satiety.

Benefits of Complex Carbs & Fiber

  • Provide sustained energy levels
  • Support digestive health and regularity
  • Aid in weight management and satiety
  • May help manage blood sugar levels
  • Contribute to a healthy heart

Types of Dietary Fats

Unsaturated Fats

Considered "healthy fats," promote heart health and may reduce inflammation.

Saturated Fats

Found in animal products and some plant sources. Consume in moderation.

Trans Fats

Unhealthy fats to limit or avoid. Often found in processed foods.

Benefits of Healthy Fats

  • Support heart health and cholesterol management
  • Promote brain function and cognitive development
  • Aid in nutrient absorption of essential vitamins
  • Contribute to a feeling of satiety and fullness
  • May support healthy skin and hair
